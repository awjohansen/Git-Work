MIZUKI NISHIYAMA

The Art of Human Fragility

MARCH 14, 2017 / ADAM JOHANSEN / PAINTING

Mizuki Nishiyama is a New York based multi-media artist, currently studying Fine Arts at Parsons School of Design.

Mizuki hopes to deliver strong statements about human vulnerability through her pieces. A representation of oneself shouldn't be restricted whatsoever. In her case, She likes to showcase a collection of her journey through paintings and poetry. The raw human passion is the ultimate quality that inspires Mizuki to create. She often works with personal experiences, ideas of the extreme, and the concept of human fragility. Through a purge of colours, an abstract expressionistic style is revealed through her work.
